Something you should know

In 2019 I sailed a 32-foot sloop solo from the Azores to Newfoundland with nothing but paper charts, a shortwave radio, and 40 kilograms of dried lentils.

The crossing took 18 days. I lost the jib on day 11, improvised a replacement from a tarp, and arrived in St. John's harbour at dawn to a single fisherman clapping from the dock. The lentils lasted exactly to the final meal.
